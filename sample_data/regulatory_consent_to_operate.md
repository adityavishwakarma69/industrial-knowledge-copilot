# Consent to Operate — Unit 1 Cooling Water and Utilities

## Summary
This document records the statutory Consent to Operate granted to Unit 1 under the Water
(Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act and the Air Act, together with the associated
monitoring obligations.

## Key Conditions
- Cooling tower CT-01 blowdown discharge must not exceed the permitted total dissolved
  solids limit; sample and log weekly.
- Maintain the surge vessel V-201 and pressure systems per statutory inspection intervals
  (cross-reference inspection report INS-NDT-2026-031).
- Pressure vessel V-201 is registered under the Factory Act; the competent-person external
  inspection certificate must remain current. Next due 2028-04-17.
- PESO licence applies to bulk storage outside this unit and is tracked separately.

## Renewal
The Consent to Operate is valid until **2027-03-31**. The renewal application must be filed
at least 120 days before expiry. Late renewal risks suspension of operations.

## Reporting
Submit the quarterly environmental statement and annual returns to the State Pollution
Control Board. Retain all monitoring records for a minimum of five years.
