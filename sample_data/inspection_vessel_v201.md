# Inspection Report: Cooling Water Surge Vessel V-201

## Asset Details
- Tag: V-201 — Cooling Water Surge Vessel
- Design code: ASME VIII Div 1, design pressure 6 barg
- Material: Carbon steel SA-516 Gr 70
- Statutory inspection interval: 2 years (external), 4 years (internal)

## Method
Ultrasonic thickness (UT) survey at 24 grid points plus external visual examination.
Equipment: digital UT gauge, calibrated against step wedge.

## Findings
- Minimum recorded shell thickness: 11.4 mm against nominal 12.0 mm (allowable minimum 9.8 mm).
- Corrosion rate estimated at 0.06 mm/year, well within limits.
- External coating breakdown observed at the bottom skirt — localised surface corrosion, no
  through-wall loss. Recommend recoating at next opportunity.
- Relief valve PSV-201 last tested 2025-10; tag and seal intact.

## Conclusion
Vessel V-201 is **fit for continued service**. Remaining life on current corrosion rate
exceeds 25 years. Next external inspection due **2028-04-17**. Address skirt coating during
the next planned shutdown.
